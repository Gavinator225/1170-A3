How To Book

I am completely booked for the month of February; however, I do not have any bookings from March till the end of the year. If you have any special events in need of catering, please check my timetable and send an email here. I will try to respond as timely as possible. Sometimes I get quite busy, so expect a response 1 - 3 business days after reaching out. If there is no response, then it is possible your email may have been buried in my inbox or someone else has already allocated that time slot. Please send a follow-up email if you get no response or if you have any other general questions. I look forward to hearing from you!
