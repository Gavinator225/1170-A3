About Me

Hi! My name is Gavin. I am an 18-year-old located in Halifax, Nova Scotia, with a passion for all things culinary related. As a chef, I offer to present an unmatched tasting experience. Special days should be spent with fun events, people you love, and most importantly, amazing food. Whether it's a wedding event in need of catering, or a Valentine's dinner in need of a personal chef, Chef Gavin has got you covered.

Why Me?

While I am busy being a full-time computer science student, I find enjoyment in cooking for others and putting a smile on people's faces. Cooking is my outlet for when things get stressful, and cooking for others helps me form connections to people and the food I make.

Chef Skills

  • I am flexible with my menu
  • I have lots of hands-on culinary experience
    • Worked at Kinton Ramen
    • Volunteered for a culinary event for Humber College, cooking and preparing food for thousands
    • Went to cooking classes since I was 10 years old
  • I can cater to small and large groups (max 15 people)
  • I can accommodate allergies and dietary restrictions
  • I bring my own cutlery, pans, and washing equipment
