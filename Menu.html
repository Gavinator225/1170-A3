Options

As a chef, it is my responsibility to provide everyone with an amazing experience, and part of that is accessibility. Whether you're a carnivore, pescatarian, or vegan, I aim to provide you with the same quality of food as the rest of the party. It is important in the email in the Book page to let me know of specific allergies and restrictions.

Seasons

I provide a seasonal menu, which means my menu changes depending on what time of the year it is. I structure my menu like this because I believe it is important to provide my customers with the freshest ingredients I possibly can. Much of my shopping is done locally, and unlike grocery stores, many of the items I cook with year-round aren't available. Despite this, it is still possible to request specific food, however, while I will try my best, I cannot guarantee the freshest ingredients.
